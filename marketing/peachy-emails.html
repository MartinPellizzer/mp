marketing ⸱ peachy emails

PEACHY

-----------------------

P = Personalize

-----------------------

1. address reader by name

- "hi, bob"

- use name 2-3 times (opening, closing)

2. mention a mutual connection

- use linkedin

- hi kim. I noticed you and I are both connected to...

3. find a similarity

- play detective

- I see that you're also a member of...

- Is that a... I have one too!

4. give a compliment

- congrats them

- be very specific with the compliment

- look for good news on the person's website or social media

5. end with a creative sign-off

- replace 'best wishes' with somethin more memorable

- make your reader smile

- wishing you a fun, productive weekend ahead.

- oh by the way... hope all goes well at [EVENT]!

- Hope your week's off to a good start, [FIRST NAME].
